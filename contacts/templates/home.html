Instructions to set up automated Gmail Access

  1. On your computer, open Gmail.
  2. Click the gear icon in the top right corner.
  3. Click All Settings.
  4. Click the Forwarding and POP/IMAP tab.
  5. In the "IMAP Access" section, select Enable IMAP.
  6. Click Save Changes.
  7. Go to your Google Account's Security Settings
  8. Enable 2-step verification
  9. Create an App password
  10. Select app -> Other
  11. Set App name as "Follow-Up Networking"
  12. Paste the “App Password” into the textbox below
  13. Press save
